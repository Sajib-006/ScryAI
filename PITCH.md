# 🛡️ Vetted — Pitch & Demo Kit

## One-liner
**Vetted is the due-diligence agent that fact-checks anything you're about to trust — and shows its sources.**

## Tagline
> *Before you trust it, vet it.*

---

## 60-second pitch (read it out loud)

> "Everyone here has been burned — a fake rental, a rug-pull token, a 'startup' that
> doesn't exist. Checking if something's legit means 10 open tabs and a gut feeling.
>
> **Vetted does it in seconds.** Paste any company, product, person, listing, or token.
> Our agent classifies it, then runs *four targeted real-time searches* across the web
> with **You.com** — reputation, risk, news, and category-specific red flags. **Claude**,
> running through **InsForge's model gateway**, weighs the evidence and returns a
> **trust score**, color-coded flags, and a verdict — every claim cited to a real source.
>
> It's a real product: **sign in**, and your entire due-diligence history is saved to
> **InsForge** — database, auth, and AI gateway, all on one platform.
>
> No hallucinations. Just sourced answers. **Don't get scammed — vet it first.**"

---

## Demo flow (90 seconds, do it live)

1. **Sign up** (5s) — "It's a real product with accounts."
2. Vet **"PEPE coin"** → watch the **search trace** run 4 You.com searches → **red gauge, High Risk**, scam flags. Click a flag's citation → real source opens.
3. Vet **"Stripe"** → **amber/green**, balanced flags. "Calibrated — legit companies aren't punished."
4. Point at the **sidebar** → "Both saved to InsForge, scoped to my account."
5. Expand **"How Vetted investigated"** → show the 4 search angles. "That's deep, real-time You.com usage per query."
6. Close on the tagline.

---

## Prize talking points (say these to judges)

**You.com ($1,000):**
- "Every verdict is grounded in **real-time You.com search** — 4 targeted queries per subject, not one."
- "Citations are first-class: every claim links to a live source. Search *is* the product."

**InsForge ($500 — Best Use):**
- "We use InsForge **three ways**: **Postgres** (report history), **Auth** (user accounts), and the **Model Gateway** (Claude for classification + verdict)."
- "One platform for data, identity, and AI — that's the agent-native cloud working end-to-end."
